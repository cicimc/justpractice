Mint Condition Tribute


Bio

Mint Condition is an Amereican R&B group from Minnesota. The band formed together in 1984. Their sound consist of jazz, hip hop, funk and dance. The group started to gain more fans in the 90's. With hits like "Pretty Brown Eyes" and "U Send Me Swingin" their singles started to reach the top. Mint Condition has been nominated for a few Grammys and Soul Train Awards. They may not be the most famous group from back in the 20th century, but their music can still be enjoyable for most R&B lovers.
